The project will be a company profile website along with a few functionalities. The basic languages used for the website will be PHP, CSS & HTML. There will also be usage of Javascript, DBMS(MySQl) etc. Firstly, There will be a homepage containing a naviagtion bar & the basic details of the company in the page body. There will be a footer for the sitemap to be shown. A few static pages(Profile, History, Media, Files, Contact etc.) will be accesible from navigation panel. There will be a static files section which will be able to display some files(PDF). A contact form which will be available in the contact us page & when form submitted, an email will be sent to the company email.
